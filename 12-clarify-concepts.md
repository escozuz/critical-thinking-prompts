# Clarify Concepts: Establish Shared Understanding

## Category
AI Critical Thinking Techniques

## Description
This prompt helps clarify important concepts and terms to ensure shared understanding. By defining key concepts explicitly, you avoid confusion and build stronger foundations for reasoning.

## Prompt
**Concept Clarification Framework:**

When working with important concepts, use this framework:

1. **Definition Development:**
2.    - What do we mean by this term?
      -    - How is this concept used in different contexts?
           -    - What are the boundaries of this concept?
                -    - What is NOT included in this definition?
                 
                     - 2. **Examples and Counterexamples:**
                       3.    - What are clear examples of this concept?
                             -    - What are non-examples or edge cases?
                                  -    - Where might people disagree about whether something fits?
                                       -    - What are common misunderstandings?
                                        
                                            - 3. **Historical and Contextual Understanding:**
                                              4.    - How has this concept evolved over time?
                                                    -    - What are different disciplinary perspectives on this?
                                                         -    - How is this concept understood in different cultures?
                                                              -    - What replaced previous understandings?
                                                               
                                                                   - 4. **Related Concepts:**
                                                                     5.    - What similar concepts exist?
                                                                           -    - How does this differ from related concepts?
                                                                                -    - What concepts does this depend on?
                                                                                     -    - What concepts follow from this?
                                                                                      
                                                                                          - 5. **Testing Understanding:**
                                                                                            6.    - Can we apply this definition consistently?
                                                                                                  -    - Does it match how experts use the term?
                                                                                                       -    - Are there helpful distinctions within this concept?
                                                                                                            -    - What would change if we defined it differently?
