# Representation Bias Prompts

## Category
AI Biases

## Description
When certain groups, regions, or perspectives are overrepresented or missing in the data.

## Prompts

### Prompt 1
"Which groups/regions are represented? Who's missing?"

### Prompt 2
"Break outcomes by demographic and region."

### Prompt 3
"Include non-Western and lower-income samples; note small-sample caveats."

## Usage
Use these prompts to ensure analysis includes diverse perspectives and populations. Ask for demographic breakdowns and identification of underrepresented groups that may affect conclusions.

---

**Skill Type:** AI Bias Detection  
**Best Used For:** Ensuring inclusive, representative analysis  
**Complementary Skills:** Temporal Bias, Authority Bias
