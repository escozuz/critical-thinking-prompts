# Root Cause Analysis: Understand Problems at the Root Level

## Category
AI Critical Thinking Techniques

## Description
This prompt helps identify root causes rather than addressing symptoms. By systematically exploring why problems occur, you can develop more effective and lasting solutions.

## Prompt
**Root Cause Analysis Framework:**

Use this structured approach to find root causes:

1. **Problem Definition:**
2.    - What exactly is the problem?
      -    - How do we know it's a problem?
           -    - Who is affected?
                -    - What are the consequences?
                 
                     - 2. **The Five Whys Technique:**
                       3.    - Why did this happen? (First why)
                             -    - Why did that cause happen? (Second why)
                                  -    - Why did that cause occur? (Third why)
                                       -    - Why did that condition exist? (Fourth why)
                                            -    - Why is that the fundamental issue? (Fifth why)
                                                 -    - Continue until you reach actual root cause
                                                  
                                                      - 3. **Causal Analysis:**
                                                        4.    - What are the direct causes?
                                                              -    - What are the contributing factors?
                                                                   -    - Which causes are within our control?
                                                                        -    - Are multiple causes at play?
                                                                         
                                                                             - 4. **Distinguishing Root from Symptom:**
                                                                               5.    - If we fix this, will the problem recur?
                                                                                     -    - Is this treating the symptom only?
                                                                                          -    - What would need to change to prevent recurrence?
                                                                                               -    - Are there hidden root causes?
                                                                                                
                                                                                                    - 5. **Systemic Perspective:**
                                                                                                      6.    - How does the broader system contribute?
                                                                                                            -    - What environmental factors play a role?
                                                                                                                 -    - How do different elements interact?
                                                                                                                      -    - Where are the leverage points?
