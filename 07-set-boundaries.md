# Set Boundaries: AI vs. Human Task Boundaries

## Category
AI Critical Thinking Techniques

## Description
This prompt defines clear boundaries between AI capabilities and human responsibilities. It helps establish appropriate task allocation in AI-assisted workflows by explicitly defining which tasks AI can handle and which require human judgment.

## Prompt
**AI vs. Human Task Boundaries Framework:**

When working with AI systems, define clear boundaries:

1. **AI-Suitable Tasks:**
2.    - Information retrieval and summarization
      -    - Pattern recognition across large datasets
           -    - Generating multiple perspectives on a topic
                -    - Drafting and organizing content
                     -    - Performing calculations and analysis
                          -    - Providing research assistance
                           
                               - 2. **Human-Required Tasks:**
                                 3.    - Final decision-making on important matters
                                       -    - Ethical judgments and value-based choices
                                            -    - Accountability and responsibility assignment
                                                 -    - Creative vision and strategic direction
                                                      -    - Relationship-building and human connection
                                                           -    - Navigating ambiguous or novel situations
                                                                -    - Expertise in specialized domains
                                                                 
                                                                     - 3. **Questions to Ask:**
                                                                       4.    - Does this task require creative vision or strategic judgment?
                                                                             -    - Who should be accountable for the outcome?
                                                                                  -    - Does this involve direct human impact or relationships?
                                                                                       -    - Is this a well-defined problem AI can help with?
                                                                                            -    - What are the consequences of getting this wrong?
