# Confirmation Bias

## Category
Human Biases

## Description
Only noticing or trusting information that agrees with what you already believe.

## Full Prompt

My current opinion: [STATE YOUR OPINION CLEARLY IN ONE SENTENCE]

Steelman the opposing view. Give me the strongest possible case against my opinion — the version a smart, well-informed person who genuinely disagrees with me would make.

Then:
1. Surface the evidence I've likely overlooked or dismissed because it doesn't fit my view
2. Identify the one assumption underneath my opinion that's most vulnerable to being wrong
3. Tell me what would have to be true for me to change my mind

Don't soften it. If my opinion has weak spots, name them directly.

---

**Skill Type:** Human Bias Detection  
**Best Used For:** Challenging your own viewpoints  
**Complementary Skills:** Availability Bias, Authority Bias
