# Identify Assumptions: Surface Hidden Assumptions

## Category
AI Critical Thinking Techniques

## Description
This prompt helps identify and surface hidden assumptions that underlie arguments, decisions, or beliefs. By making assumptions explicit, you can test them and make more informed judgments.

## Prompt
**Assumption Identification Framework:**

Use these techniques to identify hidden assumptions:

1. **Linguistic Indicators:**
2.    - Look for words like "obviously," "clearly," "of course"
      -    - Notice absolutes: "always," "never," "everyone"
           -    - Identify conditional language: "if...then" statements
                -    - Pay attention to passive voice that hides agency
                 
                     - 2. **Structural Assumptions:**
                       3.    - What does this argument take for granted?
                             -    - What needs to be true for this conclusion to hold?
                                  -    - What is presupposed rather than stated?
                                       -    - What is the underlying logical structure?
                                        
                                            - 3. **Values and Beliefs:**
                                              4.    - What values are embedded in this statement?
                                                    -    - What worldview does this assume?
                                                         -    - What definition of success is assumed?
                                                              -    - What time horizon is assumed?
                                                               
                                                                   - 4. **Testing Assumptions:**
                                                                     5.    - What would happen if this assumption were false?
                                                                           -    - Has this assumption been verified?
                                                                                -    - Are there alternative assumptions that fit the data?
                                                                                     -    - What evidence would disprove this assumption?
                                                                                      
                                                                                          - 5. **Categories of Common Assumptions:**
                                                                                            6.    - Causal: "X causes Y"
                                                                                                  -    - Predictive: "This will lead to that"
                                                                                                       -    - Value-based: "This is good/bad because..."
                                                                                                            -    - Practical: "This is how things work"
                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                 - ## Usage
                                                                                                                 - - Best Used For: Argument analysis, decision evaluation, problem solving
                                                                                                                   - - Complementary Skills: Questions That Inspire, Clarify Concepts
                                                                                                                     - - Skill Type: Critical Thinking Technique
